# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Team members are expected to communicate respectfully, review work constructively, and focus discussion on the project goals.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional and collaborative.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy, credentials, and data-use boundaries.
- Raise compliance concerns early.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or exclusionary behavior
- Sharing private credentials or sensitive data
- Adding source collection logic that violates terms of service, school policy, or legal restrictions

## Reporting

If a concern arises, contact the project lead, instructor, or designated team coordinator.
